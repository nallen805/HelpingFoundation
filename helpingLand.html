About Us

The Helping Foundation is a business that helps non-profit businesses connect with resources that will help fund their nonprofit. We work with you to get your business noticed more on the Internet, as well as a strong social media presence. From start to finish, we will come up with a plan to discuss how we can help your nonprofit business get the assistance it needs.

Our Mission

Our mission is to connect your business with other businesses that provide the resources as well as the necessary funding to help keep your business going. We connect you with companies that may already be established so that they can fund and provide helpful resources to keep your business going.
